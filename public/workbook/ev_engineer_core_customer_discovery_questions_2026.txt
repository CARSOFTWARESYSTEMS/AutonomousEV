EV.ENGINEER CUSTOMER DISCOVERY QUESTIONNAIRE
EV Battery Diagnostics & Health Intelligence
Plain Field Interview Version

Purpose:
Use these questions to understand real EV battery problems, current manual process, cost impact, urgency, and willingness to pay.

Important Instructions for Interviewer:
1. Do not sell any product during the discussion.
2. Ask questions slowly and in simple language.
3. Let the customer explain in their own words.
4. Record language used: English / Kannada / Hindi / Tamil / Mixed / Other.
5. Take photo/audio/video only with permission.
6. At the end, thank the participant politely.
7. Capture contact details separately before starting:
   - Name
   - Phone number
   - City/location
   - Category
   - EV type: 2W / 3W / 4W / Other
   - Battery type if known: LFP / NMC / Lead Acid / Unknown
   - Vehicle brand/model if applicable


============================================================
CATEGORY 1: EV OWNER / RIDER
============================================================

1. Which EV do you use? Please mention brand, model, and vehicle age.

2. How many kilometers do you usually drive per day?

3. Have you faced any battery-related problem so far?
   Examples: range drop, slow charging, battery not holding charge, overheating, sudden shutdown.

4. What is the biggest battery problem you have faced?

5. When did you first notice this problem?

6. How do you currently know whether your battery is healthy or weak?

7. Did you visit a service center for this battery issue? If yes, what did they check and tell you?

8. How much money or time did this battery issue cost you?

9. Before buying or selling a used EV, would you want a trusted battery health report? Why?

10. How much would you be willing to pay for a trusted battery health check/report?


============================================================
CATEGORY 2: EV SERVICE CENTER
============================================================

1. How many EVs do you service in a month?

2. How many customers come with battery-related complaints in a month?

3. What are the most common battery complaints you receive?

4. When a customer says range has dropped or battery is weak, how do you check the battery today?

5. What tools, software, scanner, app, or manual method do you use for battery diagnosis?

6. Can you clearly tell the customer the battery health percentage or remaining life today?

7. What is the most difficult part of battery diagnosis for your service center?

8. How do you handle battery warranty or replacement claims?

9. Can you identify battery failure before the customer faces breakdown? If yes, how?

10. Would you pay for a tool/report that gives battery health, failure risk, and remaining life? How much?


============================================================
CATEGORY 3: USED EV DEALER
============================================================

1. How many used EVs do you buy or sell in a month?

2. Which EV types do you mostly handle?
   2W / 3W / 4W / Other

3. What is the biggest concern buyers have before buying a used EV?

4. How do you currently check the battery condition before buying or selling a used EV?

5. Do buyers ask for battery health proof or warranty details?

6. Does battery condition affect the resale price? If yes, how much?

7. Have you lost any deal because of battery trust issues?

8. What battery information would help you sell used EVs faster?

9. Would a trusted battery health certificate help your business?

10. How much would you pay per vehicle for a trusted battery health certificate?


============================================================
CATEGORY 4: FLEET OPERATOR
============================================================

1. How many EVs are currently running in your fleet?

2. Which EV types are in your fleet?
   2W / 3W / 4W / Other

3. What is the average daily running distance per vehicle?

4. What are the most common battery problems in your fleet?

5. How many vehicles were unavailable last month due to battery issues?

6. How do you currently monitor battery health across the fleet?

7. Can you predict which battery may fail in the next 30 to 90 days?

8. How much money do you lose due to battery downtime or vehicle unavailability?

9. What battery information would help you reduce downtime and plan replacements better?

10. How much would you pay per vehicle per month for battery health monitoring and failure prediction?


============================================================
CATEGORY 5: BATTERY REFURBISHER / SECOND-LIFE BATTERY BUSINESS
============================================================

1. How many batteries or battery packs do you inspect, repair, or refurbish in a month?

2. Which battery types do you mostly handle?
   LFP / NMC / Lead Acid / Unknown / Other

3. From which EV types do these batteries usually come?
   2W / 3W / 4W / Other

4. How do you currently check whether a battery can be reused or refurbished?

5. What tools or machines do you use for battery testing?

6. What is the biggest challenge in deciding battery health or remaining life?

7. What are the main reasons batteries are rejected or downgraded?

8. Do you get enough history about the battery before testing it?

9. Would a digital battery health and second-life grading report help your business?

10. How much would you pay per battery pack for trusted battery health or grading support?


============================================================
CATEGORY 6: EV STARTUP / EV MANUFACTURER / BATTERY COMPANY
============================================================

1. What EV or battery product are you currently building or operating?

2. Which EV category do you focus on?
   2W / 3W / 4W / Fleet / Battery Pack / Other

3. What are the biggest battery-related problems reported by your users or customers?

4. How do you currently collect battery data from vehicles or BMS?

5. How do you currently detect battery degradation, safety risk, or failure risk?

6. What is the hardest battery problem for your engineering or support team to solve?

7. How do battery problems affect warranty, customer support, or brand trust?

8. What battery intelligence do you wish you had but do not have today?

9. Would you use an external battery diagnostics, health, or failure prediction platform?

10. What business value would such a platform create for you?


============================================================
COMMON FINAL QUESTIONS FOR ALL CATEGORIES
============================================================

Ask these only if time allows.

1. What is the single biggest battery problem we should solve first?

2. How urgent is this problem for you?
   Low / Medium / High / Critical

3. On a scale of 1 to 10, how painful is this problem?

4. Are you willing to discuss again after we prepare a solution or prototype?

5. Can we contact you later for follow-up?
